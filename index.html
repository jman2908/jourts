The Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. When you click on a crystal you will add an specific number of points to your final score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game when your final score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on the images.
Every time the game starts, the game will assign new values to each crystal.
